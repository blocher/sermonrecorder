# Unlisted Share pages with artifacts, transcript, and audio

Congregants need to email and natively share a beautiful read-only view for family or small group without granting app accounts. A Sermon may publish a Share page: unauthenticated, unlisted capability URL containing Study artifacts, Scripture references, Transcript, and audio, excluding Reflections. Links are revocable/rotatable; they are not a public SEO catalog and do not put the Sermon in another Congregant's library. We accepted higher leak risk versus artifacts-only pages in exchange for a complete handout experience.
